HOW LONG ARE YOUR CONTRACTS?

View plans are month-to-month or yearly. You can start or stop your service at any time.

HOW DO I CANCEL SERVICE?

Cancelling is easy, you can do it online, or call for our services.

CAN I SWITCH PLANS?

Yes, at any time, within your website settings. When upgrading or downgrading your website plan, you will receive either a charge or refund, depending on cost.

CAN I DOWNLOAD VIEW?

No, View is a fully-managed web service. We do not have a downloadable version. We do provide many methods for exporting your data.
